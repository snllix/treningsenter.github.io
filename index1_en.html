Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How do I register as a member?

+

You can register directly on our website by selecting your desired membership plan. You only need to fill in your contact information and choose a payment method. You get immediate access after payment is confirmed.

Can I pause my membership?

+

Yes, you can pause your membership for up to 2 months per year. Contact customer service at info@fitlife.no or call +47 22 11 22 33. There is no charge for pausing.

What equipment is available at the fitness center?

+

We have state-of-the-art equipment including: free weights, machines, cardio equipment (treadmills, bikes, cross trainers), cable apparatus, and flexibility equipment. All equipment is maintained daily and inspected monthly.

Is personal training available?

+

Yes! We offer personal training to all members. Your first consultation is free. Personal training prices start from 399 kr per hour. Quarter and year memberships include consultations.

What if I forget my ID card?

+

You can show your membership information on your phone or tell the staff your name so they can verify it in the system. We recommend downloading the FitLife app for easy access.

What are the opening hours?

+

Monday - Friday: 06:00 - 22:00
Saturday: 08:00 - 20:00
Sunday: 10:00 - 18:00

We are closed on public holidays. Questions? Call us at +47 22 11 22 33

Is there parking available?

+

Yes, we have free parking available for members in our parking basement. Parking spaces are limited, so we recommend arriving 10-15 minutes before your scheduled workout during peak hours.

What are the shower and locker room rules?

+

Showers and lockers are available for all members. Each locker requires 20 kr coin (returned). We provide free towels, and you'll find shampoo, conditioner and soap in all showers. We recommend taking your valuables with you.

Can I get a refund if I'm dissatisfied?

+

We offer a 14-day trial period for new members. If you're not satisfied, you can get a full refund within this period. After 14 days, you can cancel at any time with 30 days notice.
