🧘 Yoga Booking Guide

Learn how to easily book your yoga classes at FitLife

Welcome to Yoga at FitLife

Yoga is a wonderful way to improve flexibility, strength, and mental wellbeing. FitLife offers yoga classes for all levels, from beginners to experienced practitioners.

Class Schedule:

  • Monday: 18:00 - 19:00
  • Wednesday: 18:00 - 19:00

Step-by-Step Booking Guide

1 Log In or Create an Account

Visit the FitLife website or open the app. If you're already a member, log in with your email and password. If you're new, create an account by filling in your name, email, and phone number.

2 Go to "Classes" or "Book Time"

In the menu, find "Classes" or "Book Time" (depending on platform). Click there to see all available classes.

3 Select "Yoga" from the Class List

In the filter or class list, search for "Yoga" or select it from the categories. You'll see all yoga classes for the next 4 weeks.

4 Choose Your Preferred Class and Time

Click on a yoga class you want to attend. You will see:

  • Date and time of the class
  • Instructor's name
  • Number of available spots
  • Difficulty level (Beginner, Intermediate, Advanced)

5 Confirm Booking

Click "Book Class" or "Reserve Spot". The system will show you a confirmation screen. Review the details and click "Confirm Booking".

6 Receive Confirmation Email

You will receive a confirmation email at your registered email address with:

  • Class type and date/time
  • Instructor's name
  • Class room location
  • Reminder about the cancellation deadline

Important Information

📅 Booking Period

You can book classes up to 4 weeks in advance. Classes fill quickly during peak times.

⏰ Cancellation

Cancel at least 24 hours before the class to free up the spot for other members.

📱 Reminders

You automatically receive reminders via email and SMS (if activated) the day before the class.

🔄 Class Type

Choose classes that match your level. All classes are available for members.

💡 Helpful Tips

  • Arrive Early: Come 10-15 minutes before class starts to set up your mat and warm up
  • What to Bring: Yoga mat (or borrow free mat on-site), towel, water bottle, and comfortable clothes
  • Attend Same Class: You can book the same class every week. Click "Repeat Weekly" for automatic booking
  • Try New Classes: Experiment with different instructors and difficulty levels to find what works best
  • Cancelled Classes: If a class is cancelled, you'll be notified via email. You can automatically book another class

⚠️ Important to Note

  • If you don't show up 15 minutes after class starts, your spot may be given to others
  • Remember to cancel in time if you can't attend - it helps other members
  • If you have injuries or health concerns, inform the instructor before class starts
  • Yoga classes are included in your membership - no extra fee required
